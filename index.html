Biography

Hi I'm Yasin (Owner of TPS)

I would say I have always liked cars. My dad used to own a Toyota A70 Supra before I was born and many other cars too. It really began when I was walking home one day with my training partner and we saw a black pimped whip shooting flames out the exhaust. It didn't seem like it had that capability but there it was with the deafening bangs, but it was somehow enjoyable. The driver swung out of the car and was laughing at the top of his voice until he developed a harsh cough whilst trying to gain control again. My partner was smiling with his mouth wide open and when I got home, I decided to search on YouTube "cars shooting flames." I subscribed to many channels based around the car community that day and appreciated every video. I decided to take pictures of nice looking cars from that day onwards, despite not knowing any specific model names. Over time, I adopted the culture and was able to tell my mum the make and model of a vehicle just by hearing the sound whilst sitting in the livingroom.

Grand Turismo

One day in school, a classmate and I were speaking about cars and I mentioned my dream car to be the 1996 Toyota 2JZ-A80-GTE Supra with a TRD body revision. He told me his next door neighbour owned a Supra (Which happened to be the 1996 model) and I contacted the owner right away. The owner agreed to take me for a joy ride at 7:30pm that day. After seeing the insane acceleration Supras are capable of on Youtube, I started to feel butterflies in my stomach as we were preparing to go. When he awoke the beast and a huge roar exploded the air omnidirectionally. Even today, I judge the startup of a Supra to be the most erotic sound a petrol head could ever bear witness to. My heart began to beat faster and faster and I felt as if I was about to pee my pants. Though I didn't suppress this feeling, I sat in my seat and awaited to exit the driveway with a huge smile on my face. We drove quite sensibly going to Westfield, however he revved up at every traffic light we would stop at, turning countless heads in our direction. The majority of the people taking photos were adults, and at the time I expected nothing different, as only the elders would be aware the greatness that stood before them. We accelerated so fast my heart rate began to calm down, as if the thrill of the ride soothened my soul. On arriving back to his house, he gave me the login to a business account for the petrol head community. I owned this account until it reached 178K followers on Instagram, then we sold the account to a German buyer for an offer we simply could not refuse. I also launched a clothing brand based on JDM and many car enthusiasts purchased our apparel. It was a very successful journey which sparked the beginning of my interest in business.

Dream Car

I dream of buying a 1996 Toyota 2JZ-A80-GTE Supra and completely revising the vehicle. I plan to upgrade all internals to reach a stable minimum of 600BHP and hope to remodel the interior replacing all irreplaceable parts with new production parts. I dream of waking up to see my beauty in my garage and waking her up, appreciating every gurgle of the breathtaking machine.
